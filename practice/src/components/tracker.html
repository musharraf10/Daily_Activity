Personalized Study Roadmap

DSA Study Plan

This plan, from August 19, 2025, to December 31, 2025 (~4.5 months), focuses on building a strong foundation in DSA using Java. You should commit 2-3 hours daily, with an hour for theory and 1-2 hours for solving 2-3 problems on platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, or GeeksforGeeks. Sundays are excluded for rest or catch-up.

Key Goals 🎯

  • Master DSA fundamentals.

  • Prepare for coding interviews.
